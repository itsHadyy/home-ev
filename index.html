FAQs

Whether you're seeking expert advice, exploring our FAQs, or looking for solutions tailored to your needs, we're here to help. Our goal is to ensure you recieve insightful guidance and clarity on any topic you're curious about.

How does it work?

To rent a charger, simply download our app, create an account, and search for available chargers in your area. Select your desired charger and reservation time. Once your reservation is confirmed, you'll receive a code to access the charger.

What areas do you cover?

Our service currently covers major cities in Canada, including Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Calgary, and Edmonton. We're continuously expanding our coverage to reach more areas.

What types of chargers do you offer?

We offer both Level 2 and DC fast charging stations. Level 2 chargers are ideal for daily charging, while DC fast chargers provide rapid charging for longer journeys.

How much does it cost to rent a charger?

Our pricing is flexible and based on usage. You can choose from hourly, daily, or weekly rental plans. There are no hidden fees or surprises.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and mobile payment options. You can easily pay through our secure in-app payment system.
